WHO DARES DISTURB THE GREAT AND POWERFUL OZ?!

So, you wish to inquire about something? Perhaps a custom order? Or you simply wish to commune with Oz, the great and powerful? Well here's your chance!

If you're interested in pricing a custom job, I recommend emailing me HERE for fastest service.

Please note that any design work I have to perform prior to fabrication will incur an engineering fee at a rate of $65/hr. I can work with anything from formal blueprints to a sketch on a napkin (as long as it has the necessary measurements and properties included), but if I have to spend time figuring out dimensions, material choice, and how components come together, it's going to cost you extra!

To check on an order or for other quick questions, feel free to call me anytime!
