My name is Ozzie, and I've worked as welder/fabricator in Virginia and Washington for 7 years. Before that, I worked in the auto parts industry for an additional 7 years, which is what made me want to learn to weld and fabricate - I wanted to be able to modify and customize cars.

I learned to weld and earned my certifications from the Richmond Adult Technical Center, where I learned to stick, MIG and TIG weld. My career has been focused around TIG welding, primarily with aluminum but also stainless and carbon steels.
